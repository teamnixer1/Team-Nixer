BOOTCAMP

Coaching Support for Bronze to Platinum

🎓 What Is the Nixer Bootcamp?

The Bootcamp connects you with experienced coaches who specialise in helping players improve at specific ranks. Whether you're just starting out or nearly Diamond, we've got the right coach for your level.

🧑‍🏫 Rank-Specific Coaches:

  • Bronze Coach - Teaches core mechanics, basic movement & ball control
  • Silver Coach - Focuses on rotations, early aerials & power shots
  • Gold Coach - Sharpens decision-making, consistency, and pressure play
  • Platinum Coach - Covers advanced mechanics, positioning, and speed

🔥 What the nixer Offers:

  • ✅Free Coaching Support from Bronze to Platinum
  • 💬 Ask questions anytime in chat or during Q&A sessions
  • 🎙️ Join Bootcamp VC for live coaching, reviews, or group training
  • 🎥 Submit gameplay in #replay-submissions for personal feedback
  • 📚 Use rank-based Mechanics Guides in #mech-guides to train smarter
To gain access to most of the stuff, join our Discord Server.

📌 How to Get Involved:

No sign-ups. No forms. Just:

  • 💬 Hop in chat and ask your coach for help
  • 🎙️ Join Bootcamp VC when coaches are available
  • 🎥 Submit replays anytime for analysis
  • 🚩Train using drills from chat
To gain access to most of the stuff, join our Discord Server.

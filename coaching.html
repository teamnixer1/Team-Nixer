Game Sense Coach Requirements:

  • Rank Requirement: Must be at least Champion 1 rank in Rocket League 🚀🎮
  • Experience: Proven experience in analyzing gameplay and helping players improve strategic thinking and decision-making 📊👀
  • Knowledge: In-depth understanding of game mechanics, positioning, rotations, and map control 🧠🛠️
  • Communication Skills: Ability to explain strategies and concepts clearly and concisely to players of varying skill levels 🗣️💬
  • Patience: Capable of working with players who are still developing their game sense, offering constructive feedback and support ✨🙏
  • Leadership: Experience leading a team or working in a coaching environment, providing guidance, and fostering improvement 🏆👨‍🏫
  • Availability: Consistent availability for training sessions, coaching hours, and team strategy meetings ⏰📅

Freestyle Coach Requirements:

  • Rank Requirement: Must be at least Champion 1, with a proven track record in Freestyle Rocket League
  • Freestyle Experience: Must be highly skilled in freestyle mechanics (such as air dribbles, musty flicks, flip resets, and other advanced mechanics) 🛸🎯
  • Creativity: Ability to come up with innovative freestyle training drills and exercises 🎨⚡
  • Communication Skills: Clear ability to demonstrate and explain mechanics to players of varying levels 🗣️🤔
  • Patience and Encouragement: Willing to support players in their freestyle journey, helping them to push past plateaus and build confidence 🌱💪
  • Leadership: Ability to motivate and inspire players to achieve their freestyle goals and continuously strive for improvement 🌟🔥
  • Teamwork: Willingness to collaborate with other coaches to ensure all players improve holistically 🤝🎮
